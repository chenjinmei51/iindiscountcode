🎓 What Is the Coaching Intensive Practicum (CIP)?

The Coaching Intensive Practicum (CIP) is IIN’s most hands-on and advanced training program — a 12-week interactive experience that helps you transform from “theoretical coach” to someone confidently ready for real-world client sessions. It’s also an NBHWC-approved education program, meaning it fulfills one of the key requirements to apply for the National Board-Certified Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC) exam.

Unlike passive online courses, CIP is built around live coaching practice, mentor feedback, and real skill development. Through small-group coaching labs, buddy sessions, and expert instruction, you'll internalize the core competencies required by the NBHWC — not just understand them in theory.

📌 Who Is This For?

The Coaching Intensive Practicum is ideal for IIN Health Coach Training Program (HCTP) graduates, NBHWC-eligible health coaches, or anyone looking to gain hands-on experience before taking the board exam.

🧩 What You’ll Learn & Practice

  • Live Learning Labs: 2 mentor-led sessions per week where you apply techniques, ask questions, and build your confidence in real time.
  • Buddy Coaching: 3 sessions where you switch between coach and client roles, observed by a board-certified coach who offers direct feedback — your final session counts as a practical skills assessment (PSA).
  • NBHWC-Aligned Coaching: Learn motivational interviewing, active listening, client-centered dialogue, SMART goal setting, and ethical best practices.
  • Foundational & Advanced Modules: Weekly lessons cover coaching structure, emotional processing, cultural humility, positive psychology, behavior change, and group coaching.
  • Lifetime Access: Review 10+ hours of recorded lectures, downloadable coaching manuals, and an extensive health resource library — anytime.
